status register

bit	    description

0	    sign                        * †
1       zero                        * †
2       parity                      * †
3       carry                       *
4       borrow                      *
5       equal                           ‡
6       greater                         ‡
7       less                            ‡
8       greater (unsigned)              ‡
9       less (unsigned)                 ‡
10      mode [0 kernel / 1 user]
11      [always 1]

31-63   current instruction

* set according to result of arithmetic instruction
† set according to result of logical instruction (asr included)
‡ set according to operands of arithmetic AND logical instructions
