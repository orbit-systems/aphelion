status register

bit	    description

0	    sign                        * †
1       zero                        * †
2       parity                      * †
3       carry                       *
4       borrow                      *
5       equal                       * †
6       greater                     *
7       less                        *
8       greater (unsigned)          *
9       less (unsigned)             *
10      mode [0 kernel / 1 user]

* set according to result of arithmetic instruction
† set according to result of logical instruction (asr included)
